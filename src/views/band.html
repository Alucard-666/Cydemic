Biography

Formed in Grecia, Alajuela in 2020, Cydemic decided exploring the edge between Death Metal, Industrial Metal and Cyber sound effects focusing their lyrics on a technologically consumed world. Cydemic's sound was developed by Cyb3rd3m0n(vocals, percussion, keyboards, cyber effects) throughout making a combination between Cyber threats topics, Social Engineering and the indisputable human greed to conquer and manipulate both physical world and the cyberspace for their own purposes.
